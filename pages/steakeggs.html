Steak and Eggs Reciep

Description: This is an excellent breakfast dish, there's not too much to it, but you can add more as you like! Feel free to add more seasonings or even different types of food to spice things up to your liking!

Dificulty: Medium

Cook Time: 15 - 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Steak cut of your choice
  • Eggs
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Additional Steak Seasoning if you wish!

Cooking Steps:

  1. Set your steak out to warm up to room temperature before seasoning
  2. Once the steak is to room temperature, generously apply salt and pepper to both sides
  3. Place a pan on the stove at medium-high heat
  4. Once the pan has warmed, add in either butter or olive oil and coat the whole pan
  5. Add your steak to the pan and cook to your desired finish (we reccomend medium rare for this dish)
  6. When your steak is done, place onto a plate to rest while we cook the Eggs
  7. In the same pan, add more olive oil or butter if needed
  8. Crack 2 eggs into the pan
  9. Once the bottom of the eggs have set (roughly 2 minutes), flip them in the pan
  10. When the eggs have cooked on both sides, remove from the pan and add to the plate next to the steak
  11. Add additional seasonings per your liking and enjoy!
