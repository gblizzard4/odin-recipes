Creamy Dijon Chicken and Rice Recipe

Description: This is a great dinner recipe to impress your freinds with! It does have a few more ingredients and cooking steps than the other recipes shown but it is worth it! This recipe has a bold delicious flavor that is really brought out by the dijon mustard. This pairs great with rice but can easily be served over noodles or potatoes instead.

Dificulty: Medium

Cook Time: 30-45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Bacon
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Heavy Cream
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning

Cooking Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees farenheit
  2. Add the chicken thighs to a pan and season with salt, pepper, and Italian Seasoning.
  3. When the oven has reached temperature, please the chicken into the center rack of the oven.
  4. The chicken will need to cook in the over for roughly 25 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. Measure out 2 cups of Jasmine Rice and 2 cups of water and place into the rice cooker.
  6. Use the rice cookers automated rice cooking setting.
  7. Place a pan on the stove over medium heat.
  8. Slice the bacon into pieces and place roughly 1/2 lb into the pan.
  9. With the bacon in the pan, add roughly 1 tsp of minced garlic to the pan.
  10. Cook the bacon until it has a crispy outer texture on all sides.
  11. With the bacon in the pan, pour in 1/4 cup of white wine to deglaze the pan. Cook until it has reduced by half
  12. Next add 1 and 1/2 cups of the heavy cream to the pan
  13. Next add 2 tbsp of dijon mustard to the pan.
  14. Next add the grated parmesan cheese and stir the sauce until all the ingredients combine and begin to cook down.
  15. Add additional seasoning to the sause per your liking!
  16. Once the sauce is done, simmer until the chicken and rice finish if they have not already!
  17. Add once serving of the rice, chicken, and sauce to a plate and enjoy!
