Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich Recipe

Description: This is a simple dish, easy to make, and minimal ingredients. A true American classic recipe, this is known by almost everyone. There's not much else to it so lets dive in!

Dificulty: Easy

Cook Time: Less than 5 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Bread of your choice
  • Fruit Jelly or Jam
  • Peanut Butter

Cooking Steps:

  1. Grab 2 pieces of the bread of your choice and place on a plate
  2. Using a butter knife, spread as much or as little jelly onto one piece of bread
  3. Clean the butter knife, and then use it to spread as much or as little peanut butter onto the other piece of bread
  4. Stack the bread so that the peanut butter and jelly are in the middle, and enjoy!
